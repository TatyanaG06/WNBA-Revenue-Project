WNBA Valuation 2024

Because of the rise of the WNBA, Revenues for these teams have increased throughout the country

By Tatyana Gonzalez

December 16th 2024

T This Past WNBA season has broken records in fan attendance and viewership. With the comparison of the two revenues from 2023 and 2024, though it wasn’t a big jump for certain teams it was an increase. On the maps Indiana and New York are noticeably darker (meaning an increase in revenue). An increased revenue is important, especially for the expansion, the WNBA currently has 12 teams with two new additions coming (Golden State Valkyries (2025) and Toronto Tempo (2026), adding to the number of states with a WNBA team.

Journalist Molly Yanity spoke about the revenue increase “The increase in the fan base will cause an increase in revenue and hopefully, it will lead to better pay for these Professional Women Players”
